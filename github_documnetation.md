### 4) Text Format
1) **Headings**
* use # for heading(largest)
* use ## for sub heading(medium)
* use ### for mini heading (smallest)

2) **Bold Text**
* use ** after the text

   for example: ** sherab ** will result in **sherab**

3) **Italic Text**
* use * before and after the text.

   eg: * sherab * will result in *sherab*


4) **Bold + Italic Text**
* use *** for Bold + Italic Text

   eg:*** sherab *** wil result in ***sherab***
